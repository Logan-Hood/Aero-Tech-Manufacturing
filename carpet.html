Carpet Cleaning Mounts

Aero Tech Manufacturing designs and manufactures the industry’s most powerful, versatile and reliable PTO driven surface cleaning truck mount on the market today. The XT is designed and manufactured entirely in house in our 84,000 SQ FT facility with the help of our highly skilled team of employees to ensure the highest level of quality at the most competitive cost. We have been supplying the surface cleaning and restoration industries with factory direct sales, support, and service for over a decade throughout the United States and Canada.

Commitment to our customers:
Customer service is our upmost priority. We offer our customers with 24/6 (Mon-Sat) customer assistance with our afterhours phone line. Needing assistance on a commercial account at 2:00 am? No problem........we have you covered.
